Our story

Made by hand, made to last

Shila Handicraft began with a hook, a ball of yarn and a love of colour. From a small home studio in Lambagar, Kathmandu, every sweater, crochet piece and beaded ornament is made by hand — slowly, carefully and with a lot of heart.

When you buy from us, you are not buying from a factory. You are supporting a maker, an artisan and a craft that has been passed through generations in Nepal.
